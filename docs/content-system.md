# Coreano Content System

## 1. Purpose

Why do we create content?

Coreano의 모든 콘텐츠는
한국어를 가르치기 위한 것이 아니라
Korean Operating System을 설치하기 위해 존재한다.

---

## 2. Target Audience

Primary Audience

- Spanish speakers
- Beginner to Intermediate Korean learners
- People interested in Korean culture

Secondary Audience

- K-Drama fans
- K-pop fans
- Travelers
- Korean language teachers

---

## 3. Core Message

Every content must answer one question.

"How do Koreans think?"

Not

"What does this word mean?"

---
